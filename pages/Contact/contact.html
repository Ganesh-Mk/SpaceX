Our Story

SpaceX, founded by Elon Musk in 2002, aims to reduce space transportation costs and enable Mars colonization. The company has developed groundbreaking rockets like Falcon 9 and Falcon Heavy, and the Dragon spacecraft. SpaceX achieved the first private spacecraft to reach the ISS and is pioneering reusable rocket technology.
